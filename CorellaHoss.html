SPECIAL ABILITIES

  • Famous: As a former Foreigner, Corella can reroll 1d20 on any Social Test, and gains 1 bonus Momentum on such Tests.
  • Reputation: As an independent Colonel, Hoss' reputation is everything, granting her 3 Morale soak (each Effect=3).
  • Agent Handler (Mercenary): see Gamemaster's Guide pg. 40
  • One take, one kill: Years of combat for the cameras have honed Corella's skills and style. When making a melee or ranged attack, she can reroll any d20s which did not generate a success, and can reroll up to 4, and can add 2 bonus damage for every 1 Momentum spent.
  • Action Hero: Once per turn as a reaction, Hoss can spend 1 additional Heat to instantly move herself one zone, but becomes Dazed immediately thereafter; if already Dazed, she becomes Staggered. Additionally, when leading a fireteam, [some benefit goes here]
  • Close Personal Protection: Corella may take the Guard Reaction for 1 less heat, and gains +1 Momentum on the subsequent Guard test. Additionally, she gains 2 bonus Momentum on face-to-face Tests to determine surprise, and can reroll any dice that did not generate a success. Further, Hoss reduces all weapon range penalties by 1.
  • Life Debt: Hoss' former client list is a who's who of the Human Sphere. She can reroll 1d20 when making a Lifestyle test. Additionally, she can gain a specialist's assistance for 1 Momentum/Heat, which grants +2d20 on an eligible Test. Finally, she can reduce the restriction rating for any item or service by 2, although any items gained in this way are considered to be obtained illegally.
  • The show must go on: Corella can recover 3 stress by spending 1 Heat. Additionally, Hoss gains 1 Momentum each time she suffers a wound, and each time one of her allies is defeated. Further, Hoss can spend X Momentum to grant her allies within Close range X Morale Soak. Finally, when she gains a Condition, Corella can spend X additional Heat to Absterge as a Reaction with X bonus Momentum for the Test.
